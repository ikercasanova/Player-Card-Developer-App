# Performance Test Benchmarks — Research Notes

Values used as the "College Avg" reference line on player cards.

---

## CMJ (Countermovement Jump) — 36 cm

NCAA Division I male soccer players average 35–36 cm (age ~21).

Sources:
- Countermovement Jump Force-Time Curve Analysis (PMC 8949185)
  https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC8949185/
- Characterization of Static Strength in Soccer Players (PMC 9914659)
  https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC9914659/
- Elite Hungarian Junior Soccer Players (Tandfonline)
  https://www.tandfonline.com/doi/full/10.1080/02701367.2019.1588934

Context: U-19 = 34.5 cm, U-23 = 37.2 cm, Professional = 40.7 cm.
Flight-time methods overestimate by ~3 cm vs. force plate.

---

## Broad Jump — 220 cm

Belgian elite youth (ages 17–20) average 219–225 cm.
NCAA D1 is higher at ~241 cm, but 220 sits in the general "average" range (221–230 cm per Topendsports).

Sources:
- Lockie et al. — Division I Collegiate Soccer (PMC 5968899)
  https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC5968899/
- Deprez et al. — Belgian High-Level Youth Soccer (PubMed 25958943)
  https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/25958943/
- Topendsports Standing Long Jump Norms
  https://www.topendsports.com/testing/tests/longjump.htm
- Cartwright Fitness Standing Long Jump Averages
  https://www.cartwrightfitness.co.uk/standing-long-jump-averages/

## 30M Sprint — 4.25 s

Competitive college/adult soccer average: ~4.21–4.28 s (Ferro et al., Turkish pros).
General college-age norms: 4.3–4.4 s (Cartwright Fitness).
Set at 4.25 s — credible to coaches, competitive range.

Sources:
- Ferro et al. — Speed Performance in Soccer (PMC 4327365)
  https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC4327365/
- Raków Academy Normative Data (PMC 11751035)
  https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC11751035/
- SpeedEndurance — Soccer 30M Sprint Test
  https://speedendurance.com/2010/10/10/the-soccer-football-30-meter-sprint-test/
- Cartwright Fitness 30M Sprint Normative Data
  https://www.cartwrightfitness.co.uk/30m-sprint-test-normative-data/

## 40YD Dash — 4.85 s

Nike SPARQ 50th percentile: ~4.92 s. SPARQ 65th percentile: ~4.80 s.
Set at 4.85 s — between 50th and 65th percentile, credible to coaches.

Sources:
- Nike SPARQ Soccer Scoring Table (West Side Alliance)
  https://wsasoccer.demosphere-secure.com/training-resources/nike-sparq-training/computing-my-score
- First Down Training — Average 40 Time by Age
  https://www.firstdowntraining.com/post/average-40-time-based-on-age
- Topendsports — 40-Yard Dash Test
  https://www.topendsports.com/testing/tests/sprint-40yards.htm

Note: Soccer does not standardize the 40yd dash. MLS Combine used 30m.
No large published dataset exists specifically for college soccer 40yd times.
